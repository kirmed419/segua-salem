02 About

A statistician who likes to ship — from the math underneath to the system that runs.

I'm a Master's graduate in Statistics & Data Science and a State Engineer in Statistics from ENSSEA in Algiers. My training is the rigorous side — econometrics, modeling, experimental design — but my favourite work is taking that rigour all the way to something deployed.

Across two theses and an internship at Djezzy, I've built a deep-learning system that reads the road, an AI compliance platform, and clustering models that reshaped a distribution strategy. I care that a result holds up out of sample, and that a non-technical reader still follows the story.

Outside the models, I've guided tourists, coordinated logistics and designed visuals — which is probably why I can't help making the charts look good too.
